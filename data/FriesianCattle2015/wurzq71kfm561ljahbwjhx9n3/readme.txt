FriesianCattle 2015 Dataset
---------------------------
(associated to paper: W Andrew, T Burghardt, S Hannuna, N Campbell. Automatic Individual Holstein Friesian Cattle Identification via Selective Local Coat Pattern Matching in RGB-D Imagery. IEEE International Conference on Image Processing (ICIP). September 2016.)

CONTENTS:
The training dataset (Cows-training folder) consists of 
10 individuals, 83 images and the testing dataset (Cows-testing folder)
consists of 40 individuals, 294 images.

FILE FORMAT:
standard png images

DESCRIPTION:
Depth-segmented RGB images of Friesian cattle used for feature extraction.
